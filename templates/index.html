Got Happy?

Each year since 2012, the WHR publishes its World Happiness Report - relying on various sources of data, most notably data from the Gallop World Poll - which is an annual report that illustrates how citizens across the world perceive their levels of happiness and quality of life.

Residents from over 150 countries have participated in this survey. A significant data point comes from responses from the Cantril Self-Anchoring Striving Scale.

Did you know? The World Happiness Report (WHR) is a publication of the Sustainable Development Solutions Network.
